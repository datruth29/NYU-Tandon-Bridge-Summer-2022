## Formal Definition

$T(N) = O(f(N))$ if there are positive constants $c$ and $n_0$ such that $T(N) \leq cf(N)$ when $N \geq n_0$

The growth rate of $T(N)$ is $less than or equal to$ that of $f(N)$.
