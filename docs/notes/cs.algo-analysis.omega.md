## Formal Definition

$T(N) = \Omega(g(N))$ if there are psitive constants $c$ and $n_0$ such that $T(N) \geq cg(N)$ when $N \leq n_0$.

The growth rate of $T(N)$ is __greater than or equal to__ that of $g(N)$.
